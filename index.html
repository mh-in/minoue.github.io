About

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Greifswald in the working group of Prof. Dr. Martin Schnittler.

My research fields are molecular ecology and phylogeography. Currently, I am working with slime moulds (Myxomycetes) and club mosses (Diphasiastrum, Lycopodiaceae).
